About Gifted

Gifted is a fun way to share and discover GIFs with friends. Create gifts with hidden GIFs inside, and open gifts from others to reveal their contents!

How it works:

  1. Create a gift by adding a tag (description) and selecting a GIF
  2. Other users can see your gift's tag but not the GIF inside
  3. When someone opens your gift, they'll see the GIF you chose!
